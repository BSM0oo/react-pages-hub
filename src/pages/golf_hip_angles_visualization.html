The Revolutionary Insight: Professional golfers don't avoid early extension - they use it strategically on one side while the other side does the opposite. This creates natural rotation and power.

Common Error: Most amateur golfers try to drive both hips back simultaneously, which kills rotation and creates a steep, unnatural swing plane.

The Fix: Allow your trail side to "early extend" in the downswing while your lead side flexes - this is natural and powerful movement that every tour professional uses.
