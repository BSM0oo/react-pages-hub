Blower Door & Envelope Retrofit – 20-Question Quiz

1. What is the industry-standard pressure for blower-door tests?

Correct answer: 50 Pa – the reference pressure for ACH calculations.

2. Which unit is used to measure air leakage during a blower-door test?

Correct answer: CFM – cubic feet per minute of airflow.

3. ACH50 stands for ___.

Correct answer: Air Changes per Hour at 50 Pa.

4. Which ACH50 value meets Passive House airtightness?

Correct answer: 0.6 ACH50 – Passive House limit.

5. Which item is a frequent cause of excessive leakage into attics?

Correct answer: Recessed lighting – often not air-tight rated.

6. The main goal of air sealing is to ___.

Correct answer: Decrease uncontrolled air leakage.

7. A common leakage site at floor level is the ___.

Correct answer: Rim joist – perimeter framing at the edge of the floor.

8. A high ACH50 value indicates ___.

Correct answer: Significant air leakage.

9. For many 1980s homes, a realistic post-retrofit target is below ___ ACH50.

Correct answer: 7 ACH50 – meets many retrofit code requirements.

10. Which option will NOT reduce envelope leakage?

Correct answer: Replacing light bulbs.

11. Depressurizing the house during a blower-door test helps ___.

Correct answer: Reveal leakage paths by pulling outside air through cracks.

12. Which energy code sets ≤ 5 ACH50 for Climate Zone 2?

Correct answer: IECC 2021.

13. A common ceiling-level bypass is ___.

Correct answer: Recessed lights often vent to attics.

14. What material is typically sprayed to seal attic top plates?

Correct answer: Two-part spray foam creates an air-tight seal.

15. Which retrofit often yields the largest ACH50 drop for modest cost?

Correct answer: Crawlspace liner & encapsulation.

16. An insulated attic hatch primarily prevents ___.

Correct answer: Air leakage between house and attic.

17. Cutting ACH50 from 12 to 6 can reduce HVAC load roughly ___.

Correct answer: About 20 % load reduction.

18. Which diagnostic test isolates duct leakage after a blower door?

Correct answer: Duct blaster pressurizes ducts separately.

19. ENERGY STAR new-home programs typically require ≤ ___ ACH50.

Correct answer: 3 ACH50.

20. What is the simplest DIY air-sealing task?

Correct answer: Caulking baseboards – quick, cheap, and effective.
